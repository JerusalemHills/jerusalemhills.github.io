Welcome Parents & Teachers!

Jerusalem Hills Kids is a safe, educational corner of the Jerusalem Hills website designed specifically for children. We believe learning should be fun, engaging, and most importantly - safe.

Our Commitment to Safety

No Ads No Tracking No External Links 100% Safe

What Makes Us Safe?

  • Zero Advertising: No ads, pop-ups, or commercial content
  • No Data Collection: We don't track, collect, or store any personal information
  • No External Links: Kids stay within our safe, curated environment
  • Age-Appropriate Content: All games and content designed for children ages 5-12
  • No Sign-Up Required: Play instantly without creating accounts
  • 100% Free: All games are completely free to play

Educational Benefits

Math Quest

Helps children develop:

  • Basic arithmetic skills (addition, subtraction)
  • Mental math abilities
  • Number recognition and quick thinking

Word Builder

Encourages:

  • Vocabulary development
  • Spelling practice
  • Pattern recognition
  • Problem-solving skills

Memory Match

Strengthens:

  • Short-term memory
  • Concentration and focus
  • Visual recognition
  • Cognitive development

How to Use

Kids can play independently or with parent/teacher supervision. All games are:

  • Self-paced: No time pressure or stress
  • Encouraging: Positive feedback for all attempts
  • Repeatable: Play as many times as you like
  • Mobile-Friendly: Works on tablets, phones, and computers

Privacy Policy

Jerusalem Hills Kids respects children's privacy:

  • We do not collect any personal information
  • We do not use cookies or tracking technologies
  • We do not share data with third parties
  • All games run entirely in your browser - nothing is sent to servers
  • No registration or accounts required
