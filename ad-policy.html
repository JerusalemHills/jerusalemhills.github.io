Advertising Policy

Overview

Jerusalem Hills is committed to providing a high-quality experience for both our users and advertisers. This policy outlines our standards and requirements for all advertisements displayed on our platform.

By purchasing advertising on Jerusalem Hills, advertisers agree to comply with this policy in full. Jerusalem Hills reserves the right to reject or remove any advertisement that does not comply with these guidelines at any time.

Content Standards

All advertisements must:

  • Be accurate, honest, and truthful
  • Clearly identify the advertiser and the product or service being promoted
  • Not contain deceptive, false, or misleading claims
  • Not promote illegal activities or products
  • Not contain offensive, inappropriate, or adult content
  • Not contain hate speech or discriminatory content
  • Not infringe on any third-party intellectual property rights
  • Comply with all applicable laws and regulations

Prohibited Content

Jerusalem Hills does not accept advertisements for:

  • Tobacco, e-cigarettes, or vaping products
  • Weapons, firearms, or ammunition
  • Adult or sexually explicit content
  • Gambling services (unless legally permitted in the target jurisdiction)
  • Get-rich-quick schemes or pyramid programs
  • Cryptocurrency offerings or initial coin offerings (ICOs)
  • Political campaigns or advocacy (case-by-case consideration)
  • Content that disparages competitors
  • Illegal products or services

Jerusalem Hills reserves the right to reject any advertisement that we deem inappropriate for our audience.

Animated Ads

  • Animations should not exceed 15 seconds in length
  • Animations should not loop more than 3 times
  • Animations should not contain rapidly flashing content

Performance Guidelines

  • Ads must not negatively impact site performance
  • Ads must not interfere with site functionality
  • Ads must be responsive and display correctly on all devices
  • Ads must not automatically download files to user devices
  • Audio in ads must be user-initiated (click to play)

Ad Tracking & Privacy

All advertisements must:

  • Comply with applicable privacy laws including GDPR and CCPA where relevant
  • Provide clear disclosure when collecting user data
  • Not use invasive tracking methods
  • Not track sensitive personal information
  • Include a privacy policy that is easily accessible to users

Jerusalem Hills reserves the right to reject advertisements that use tracking technologies we deem overly intrusive.

Ad Review Process

All advertisements submitted to Jerusalem Hills undergo a review process before publication:

  1. Initial submission of creative materials and landing pages
  2. Review for compliance with our advertising policy
  3. Feedback provided if changes are needed
  4. Final approval and scheduling

Please allow 2-3 business days for the review process. Rush reviews may be available upon request.

Cancellation Policy

Cancellation requests must be submitted in writing at least 5 business days prior to the scheduled start date. Cancellations after this period may be subject to a cancellation fee.

Contact Information

For questions about our advertising policy or to submit advertisements for review, please contact:

Email: advertising@jerusalemhills.com

Policy Updates

Jerusalem Hills reserves the right to update this policy at any time. Advertisers will be notified of significant changes, and continued advertising on our platform constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
