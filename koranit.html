Exploring the Beauty and Benefits of 'Koranit'

October 25, 2023 Jerusalem Hills

Mediterranean Wild Thyme, locally known as 'Koranit' or 'Zucheif,' has been a cherished ingredient in the culinary and traditional medicine of Jerusalem. This aromatic herb has a rich history and offers a myriad of benefits. Join us as we delve into the world of 'Koranit' and explore its cultural and health significance.
