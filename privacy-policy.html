Privacy Policy

Effective Date: October 19, 2025

1. Information We Collect

Overview: Jerusalem Hills is committed to privacy-first practices. We do not use cookies for tracking or advertising personalization. Participation in games, forums, or other activities on this site is entirely optional and anonymous.

Google Analytics (Cookie-less): We use Google Analytics 4 with cookie storage completely disabled. This means:

  • No cookies are set on your device
  • We collect anonymous, aggregated data: page views, referral sources, device types, and general location (city level)
  • IP addresses are anonymized before processing
  • No cross-site or cross-device tracking
  • You cannot be identified as a returning visitor

AdSense Limited Ads (Optional): If we enable advertising, we use Google AdSense Limited Ads which minimizes data collection:

  • No personalized advertising (ads are contextual based on page content only)
  • Minimal technical data collected for fraud prevention only
  • No tracking cookies for ad personalization
  • IP address may be used for geolocation (country/region level only)

Payment Processing: When you make a purchase, you are redirected to Stripe's secure checkout page (checkout.stripe.com). Stripe may use cookies on their domain for payment processing and fraud prevention. We do not store your payment information—it is handled exclusively by Stripe.

2. Stripe Payment Processing

We use Stripe, Inc. for secure payment processing. When you make a purchase on our marketplace:

  • Redirect to Stripe: You are redirected to Stripe's hosted checkout page (checkout.stripe.com)
  • Stripe's Cookies: Stripe uses cookies on their domain for session management, fraud prevention, and payment processing
  • No Data Storage: We do not store your credit card information or payment details
  • Secure Processing: All payment data is encrypted and processed by Stripe in compliance with PCI DSS standards

What Stripe Collects:

  • Payment information (credit card, billing address)
  • Transaction details (amount, currency, purchase description)
  • Device and browser information for fraud prevention

For detailed information about Stripe's data practices, please review:

  • Stripe Privacy Policy
  • Stripe Cookies Policy

We receive only transaction confirmation and order details from Stripe—no payment information is shared with us.

3. Your Rights and GDPR Compliance

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights under applicable data protection laws, including the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) if you are in the European Union:

  • Right to Access: Request information about what personal data is collected and how it's used
  • Right to Rectification: Request correction of inaccurate personal data
  • Right to Erasure: Request deletion of personal data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to Restrict Processing: Request limitation of data processing under certain conditions
  • Right to Data Portability: Request transfer of data to another service provider
  • Right to Object: Object to data processing, particularly for marketing purposes
  • Right to Opt-Out: Opt out of personalized advertising

Managing Your Advertising Preferences:

  • Visit Google's Ad Settings to control personalized ads
  • Use Digital Advertising Alliance's opt-out tool
  • Visit Network Advertising Initiative's opt-out page
  • Enable "Do Not Track" in your browser settings

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at the email address provided below. We will respond to your request within the timeframe required by applicable law.
